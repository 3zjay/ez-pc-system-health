Querying HWiNFO requires Windows and HWiNFO application to be running with registry gadget exporting enabled.

How to Enable HWiNFO Gadget Exporting (Windows):

  1. Open HWiNFO64.
  2. Click on Sensors to open the Sensor Status window.
  3. Click the gear icon (Settings) at the bottom right.
  4. Navigate to the HWiNFO Gadget tab.
  5. Select the sensors you want to monitor, and check "Report value in Gadget".
  6. Click OK. The sensors will now stream to your dashboard in real-time.
